# Korea–Uzbekistan Business Expansion Analysis

Personal research project tracking Korean companies expanding into, or already operating in, Uzbekistan — with a focus on identifying growth sectors and potential employers for a Korean-speaking, Uzbekistan-based job seeker.

## Goal

Build and maintain a structured dataset of Korean companies with a presence or active project in Uzbekistan, covering:

- Company profile (name, Korean HQ, established year, employees, revenue/profit)
- Their Uzbekistan project (investment amount, type, status, location)
- Sources, so every data point can be re-verified later

## Scope

Sectors of interest (not exhaustive — add more as you discover them):

- Automotive & auto parts
- Semiconductors / electronics
- Healthcare & pharmaceuticals
- Energy, oil & gas, mining
- Textile & cotton value chain
- Construction & infrastructure
- IT / software / telecom
- Banking & finance
- Agriculture & food processing
- Logistics & trade
