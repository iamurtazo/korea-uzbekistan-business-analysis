# Search Strategy

## General approach

Use both English and Korean search engines. Korean corporate press releases and KOTRA reports are often poorly indexed in English search engines, while English/Uzbek sources cover the Uzbekistan side of the story better. Cross-check both.

## Priority data sources (start here)

1. **KOTRA Tashkent Trade Center (KOTRA 타슈켄트 무역관)**
   - https://dream.kotra.or.kr — search "해외시장뉴스" and "국가정보"
   - Search "우즈베키스탄" and look for articles containing "진출", "투자", "MOU", "협력"
   - KOTRA periodically publishes country profiles ("국가정보") with sector breakdowns of Korean investment — this is one of the richest single sources available.

2. **Korea Eximbank overseas direct investment statistics (해외직접투자통계)**
   - https://stats.koreaexim.go.kr
   - Filterable by country (Uzbekistan) and industry. Includes investor company name, investment amount, industry code, and often the date. This is the best quantitative source for the "Investment Amount" column.

3. **DART — Korean corporate disclosure system**
   - https://dart.fss.or.kr
   - For KOSPI/KOSDAQ-listed companies, search disclosures containing "우즈베키스탄". Large overseas investments must be publicly disclosed, often with the exact amount and purpose.

4. **Local Uzbek news covering Korean investment**
   - gazeta.uz, kun.uz, uzdaily.uz, podrobno.uz, uza.uz (Uzbekistan National News Agency)
   - Uzbekistan Ministry of Investment, Industry and Trade: https://invest.gov.uz
   - Search (English): "Korean investment Uzbekistan", "South Korea Uzbekistan MOU", "Korean company Tashkent"

5. **Korean business news**
   - Yonhap News (연합뉴스), 매일경제, 한국경제, 서울경제, 이데일리, 조선비즈
   - Search (Korean): "우즈베키스탄 투자", "우즈베키스탄 진출", "우즈베키스탄 협력", "우즈베키스탄 공장"

6. **Naver-specific tips**
   - Use the Naver News tab (news.naver.com), not just general web search — much better recall for corporate press releases.
   - Combine company name + country: "삼성 우즈베키스탄", "현대자동차 우즈베키스탄", "SK 우즈베키스탄", "포스코 우즈베키스탄", "LG 우즈베키스탄"
   - Try local-entity phrasing: "OOO 우즈베키스탄 법인" (local subsidiary), "OOO 우즈베키스탄 지사" (branch office)

- POSCO textile joint ventures
- KOGAS gas exploration projects
- Korea National Oil Corporation projects
- Korea Development Bank / Korea Eximbank project financing in Uzbekistan

## Process tip

When you find a company, always try to check:

1. Its Korean HQ investor relations / newsroom page (annual reports usually list employee count and revenue).
2. Its LinkedIn page — sometimes lists an Uzbekistan office and headcount.
3. Uzbek job boards (hh.uz, jobs.uz) for postings under the company's Uzbekistan entity name — this doubles as evidence of an active local operation and as a job lead for you.
